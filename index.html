Why Vrbas Construction?

Vrbas Construction is a local construction company that is owned and operated by Thomas Vrbas for over 25 years. Tom has spent more than 30 years in the residential construction industry with a focus on homes on or near Whidbey Island. Carpentry began at a young age where he was taught the trade by his father where he helped build many houses and farm buildings in southwestern Nebraska.

In the years since then, Tom has worked on a wide array of projects ranging from window installation to performing all the fine finish carpentry work in multi-million dollar homes. The entire time Tom has built his reputation as being one of the best of what he does and ensuring that the homeowner gets the best possible results for their money.
